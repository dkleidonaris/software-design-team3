About CalPal

CalPal is a simple and intuitive calorie and meal tracking application created by a team of students from the University of Thessaly as part of a semester project for a course on Software Design and Development.

About the App

CalPal is a web-based calorie tracking tool that helps users:

  • Log meals and snacks quickly and easily
  • Track daily calorie intake
  • View a history of meals by date
  • Set calorie goals
  • Stay on track with healthy eating habits
